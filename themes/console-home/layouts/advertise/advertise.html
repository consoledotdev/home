How to work with us

1 / Free inclusion and review

Each week, Console reviews 2-3 interesting tools that we recommend to our subscribers. Subscribers trust and value our recommendations. We do not take payment for inclusion and do not charge to consider a product.

2 / Editorially independent

Reviews are editorially independent and partners do not get to read the review before it is published. Only the best tools make it and our readers know that if a tool is featured, it’s worth trying out. This means that we only include tools that meet our selection criteria.

3 / Focused advertising

The packages below are only available once we have completed a successful review. Each campaign is unique, built from the ground up for every partner. This is not generic advertising - we put you in front of experienced developers specifically looking to try new tools. If you are interested in either package, please get in touch so we can prioritize the evaluation of your product.
